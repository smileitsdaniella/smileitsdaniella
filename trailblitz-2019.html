Trail Blitz Photographs


----

Intro

For a booklet assignment I was tasked with taking photographs based on California Bay Area cities' greenspaces for a "travel company" called Trail Blitz. The photos would be laid out on a eight page booklet spread, highlighting three greenspaces, and would encourage people to visit these places.

Greenspace

Greenspace is a place that is usually undeveloped open space; for example: parks.

Inspiration

When taking these photos I decided to take some close up shots to showcase the beauty of even the smallest details that exist in nature. Such as the unique ridges in the tree's bark. The other photographs I took were of the "big picture" with nearly everything in the frame.

Location

City: Newark, CA.
Three greenspaces: Lake Shore Park, Byington Park, and Newark Community Park




Camera Used

-Canon EOS Rebel T6 DSLR -18-55mm Lens
