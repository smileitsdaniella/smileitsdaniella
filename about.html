Hello

My name is Danielle Lowe, a Graphic Designer with a BA in Graphic Design from California State University, East Bay.

I enjoy creating digital illustrations such as characters and flowers. I love to learning new art techniques and using different tools to create art, with my main strong points being in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign.

My process starts with sketches that are brought into Illustrator to be developed digitally. Born and raised in Bay Area, California I draw my inspiration from the diverse city of cultures. I aim to inspire others by adding color and life to my art.
