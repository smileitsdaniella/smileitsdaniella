Danielle Lowe

Hello

My name is Danielle Lowe, a Graphic Designer currently studying for my BA in Graphic Design at California State University, East Bay. I develop logos and enjoy helping people design their identifible personal style.

I enjoy creating digital illustrations such as characters and flowers. I love to learning new art techniques and using different tools to create art, with my main strong points being in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign.

My process starts with sketches that are brought into Illustrator to be developed digitally. I draw inspiration from the diverse city of cultures, since I was born and raised in the Bay Area, California. I aim to inspire others in bringing characters and environments to life sparked by creativity.
