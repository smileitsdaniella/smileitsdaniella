Ayeesha's Endcard

----



Endcard

A screen that Youtube viewers will see at the last few seconds of a Youtube video. A consistent screen that viewers will see at the end every video that the Youtuber creates. The Youtuber uses this screen to showcase other linked previous videos, as well as their icon link for viewers to subscribe to their Youtube channel.

Intro

When I was originally tasked with creating an end card for my friend: Ayeesha, I was excited to be able to work with a client to help their vision come to life. At first, Ayeesha gave me creative freedom in the intial drafts, since she wasn't sure what she wanted on her endcard.

Inspiration

My original idea was to incorporate Disney's Princess Jasmine aspects towards the design, since Jasmine is Ayeesha's favorite Disney princess and the princess she relates to the most. First I looked to Princess Jasmine's color palette: sea green foam and yellow gold.



Feedback

After recieving feedback from the client we decided to make the final piece with a black background rather than white.
