1.27.17
Coffee in Sf

On this day we ventured out to the city, San Francisco. I was taking an introduction to photography class; the assignment for the week was to go into the city and take 1,000 photos. We decided to stay in San Francisco for the day. It was a chilly morning, so we stopped by a small donut shop that was close by. I got this beautiful shot of the hot coffee as the first photo out of the 1,000 that was needed. As the day went on we walked around enjoying the building's architecture and the lovely day.
